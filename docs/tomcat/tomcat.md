## tomcat

```shell
## 关闭端口
<Server port="${getDeployConfig(app_tomcat_shutdown_port)}" shutdown="SHUTDOWN">
## http访问端口
<Connector port="${getDeployConfig(app_tomcat_http_port)}" protocol="HTTP/1.1"
```
